Film and cast

Examples from the series

Dr. No

Ursula Andress played Honey Ryder, one of the most famous early Bond girls.

From Russia with Love

Daniela Bianchi played Tatiana Romanova, with Lotte Lenya appearing as Rosa Klebb.

Goldfinger

Honor Blackman played Pussy Galore, a defining name in the Bond series.

Thunderball

Claudine Auger played Domino, paired with Luciana Paluzzi as Fiona Volpe.

Live and Let Die

Jane Seymour played Solitaire, alongside Madeline Smith as Miss Caruso.

GoldenEye

Izabella Scorupco played Natalya Simonova, and Famke Janssen played Xenia Onatopp.
